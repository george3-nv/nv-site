How

Every incubation starts with a product innovation. Then, we ask ourselves two questions: who is the best operator in the world suited to build this, and who is the audience best suited to buy it? We pair the best builder with the highest-converting attention. The result is a company that reaches its customer on day one.

Why

We have become a society where trust is driven by individuals. We believe each other. We buy from each other. Any brand that wants to grow has to earn its way into that trust and reach customers through the voices they already follow. That’s why we build companies with attention.
